Privacy Notice

MonadBox is an open-source Android client. This app is provided as-is and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

Data Collection

- No third-party analytics SDKs.
- No third-party crash reporting SDKs.
- No cloud update SDKs that upload your usage data.
- Runtime logs stay on your device unless you explicitly export or share them.

Permission Usage

- INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE:
	Used for proxy runtime networking, profile download, and network checks.
- POST_NOTIFICATIONS:
	Used for runtime and service status notifications.
- CAMERA:
	Optional, only for QR code import.
- GET_INSTALLED_APPS:
	Used on supported devices for access-control and RootTun app filtering. Package lists are processed locally.
- QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES:
	Used to map VPN connections back to their source apps and to support app-based routing features. Package visibility is processed locally and not uploaded.
- Foreground service permissions:
	Required by Android for VPN and proxy background services.

MonadBox does not upload your installed app list, profile files, or runtime logs to an app-owned cloud backend.

File Access And Sharing

- Import/export is user-triggered.
- Sharing uses Android system sharing (FileProvider) only for files you explicitly choose to export.

Cookies

MonadBox itself does not use web cookies. Third-party websites opened from app links may use their own cookie and privacy policies.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect local data, but no method can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

MonadBox is not specifically directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes To This Notice

This notice may be updated over time. Updates are effective after publication in repository and app resources.

Contact

If you have questions or suggestions about this privacy notice, contact project maintainers via repository issues.
